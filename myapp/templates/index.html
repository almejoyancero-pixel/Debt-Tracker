About Debt Tracker System

Debt Tracker System is designed especially for small businesses and sari-sari store owners who still manage debts using notebooks or verbal agreements.

With this system, creditors can list every utang, monitor who still has balance, and confirm payments with just a few clicks. Debtors can also see their current balance, past payments, and the status of their debts anytime.

By putting all records in one secure place, the Debt Tracker System helps avoid miscommunication, arguments, and lost records, making it easier to maintain good relationships between store owners and customers.
